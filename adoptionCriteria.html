Stray Adoption

Adoption Criteria for Stray Animals:

1. Willingness and ability to provide a loving and caring home for the adopted animal.

2. Age requirement: Must be 18 years of age or older.

3. Agree to the terms and conditions of adoption, acknowledging that not taking proper care of the adopted animal may result in 2 years of imprisonment and a penalty of 2 lakh.

4. Sufficient time commitment to spend with the adopted animal, including regular feeding, exercise, grooming, and veterinary care.

5. Provide a safe and secure living environment, including appropriate shelter and containment to prevent the animal from becoming lost or endangered.

6. Ensure that all existing pets in the household are up to date on vaccinations and are well socialized with other animals.

7. Agree to license and microchip the adopted animal for identification and ensure that the contact information is kept up to date.

8. Financial capability to meet the costs associated with pet ownership, including food, vaccinations, routine medical care, and unexpected veterinary expenses.

9. Commitment to spaying or neutering the adopted animal, in order to prevent overpopulation and reduce the number of stray animals.

10. Willingness to provide necessary training and behavioral guidance to the adopted animal, if required.

11. Agree to allow follow-up visits from authorized representatives to ensure the well-being of the adopted animal.

12. Understand that the animals available on the adoption website are free, but there may be applicable fees for licensing, microchipping, or other services.

By accepting these criteria, you are demonstrating your commitment to providing a loving home for a stray animal and accepting the responsibility that comes with it.
